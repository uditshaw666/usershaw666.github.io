PERINATAL MOOD AND ANXIETY DISORDERS

THE MAIN DIAGNOSES


The term Perinatal Mood And Anxeity Disorders is used to ecompass Depression in the pre-partum(before),anterpartum
(during) and pospartum (after) periods and affects approximatly 1 in 7 woman for each pregnancy experience.While
"Pospartum Depression" is the most known, "postpartum" in fact "after pregnancy".

DEPRESSION

One of several mental illness affecting 20% of mothers during and /or after pregnancy.

 


learn more >>

ANXIETY

Pospartum Anxeity affects more than 10% of pregnant and pospartum women. Racing,intrusive thoughts are common and worsen if untreated.

 

learn more >>

OBSESSIVE COMPULSIVE DISORDER (OCD)

Symptoms are common,but unwanted and repetitive. Intrusive thoughts and behaviors interfere with daily life and functioning.

 

learn more >>

POSTTRAUMATIC STRESS DISORDER (PTSD)

Resulting from realand preceived Perinatal trauma, PTSD is debilitating illness affecting over 10% of women and partners.

 

learn more >>

POSTPARTUM PSYCHOSIS

The most rare diagnosis affecting 0.1%-0.2% of postpartum women,usually within the first 2 weeks.

 

learn more >>

BIPOLAR (BPD) AND MOOD DISORDERS

A Bipolar Mood Disorder is characterized by periods ofextreme highs (hypomania/mania) and lows (depression).
